# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Share context and evidence when raising concerns.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Trolling, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate these standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Reporting
Report violations to `security@duckcode.ai` with relevant context, links, and timestamps. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Maintainers may apply any action deemed appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from project spaces.
